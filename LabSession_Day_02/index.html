Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

Missile Man of India • Visionary • People's President

Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam was more than a scientist or president — he was a soul stitched with dreams and devotion. Born into humble beginnings, he rose to become a beacon of inspiration for youth across India. His life reminds us that wings of fire grow from minds ignited with purpose.

Why I Admire Him

  • He turned science into poetry — leading India’s space and missile programs with brilliance.
  • He taught us that true strength lies in humility and curiosity.
  • His books like *Wings of Fire* and speeches still spark fire in a million hearts.
